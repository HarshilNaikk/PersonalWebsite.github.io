More About Me

I'm a 20 Year old guy who goes regularly drools over Drones and Punjabi Cuisine. Find me either diving deep into a robotics project, or building something cool with my own hands (a.k.a pottery, knitting, sculpting)

Howdy!

I'm from the city of Surat, Gujarat. I live in Mumbai, where I'm pursuing my B.Tech in Aerospace Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay.

I've been involved in a lot of projects and technical work in the domains of Control and Motion planning of Autonomous vehicles. I would love to expand my domains, and am always looking for the next big opportunity to challenge myself and my skills.

Drones and aerial vehicles have always fascinated me, and that is what got me into robotics. I've participated and won in quite a few International Robotics competition, gaining a lot of exposure, and learning lots of new skills in the process.

My Work Experience.

June 2021 - Present

Manager

Team AeRoVe

Member of the team in charge of planning, organizing and publicizing events under the Innovation Cell. I also moderate and channel information between the Core team members and the Business team members. I have Implemented initiatives to facilitate communication, ease workflow, and to ensure documentation in the team. I also helped in procuring a yearly budget of 1.5 million INR to the Student Technical Projects Committee, IRCC.

September 2019 - June 2021

Jr. Control and Motion Planning engineer

Team AeRoVe

During my time as a Junior Engineer, I learnt about the basics of Flight Control, and Motion Planning. I even implemented lots of SITL Simulations using ROS-Gazebo, and even participated in the Barcelona Smart Drone Challenge, where we manufactured multiple trainer aircrafts, and tested various autonomous maneuvers like Dynamic Waypoint navigation and Geolocation. We even participated in the IARC Simulation Challenge 2020, where I simulated the complete mission in PX4-SITL. We were crowned WORLD CHAMPIONS for the challenge.

June 2021 - Present

Motion Planning Subsystem Head

Team AeRoVe

I currently lead the Motion planning subsystem to build and validate frameworks and planning pipelines that drive our Drone in the International Aerial Robotics Competition hosted by AUVSI. I've led the development of various Global and Local planning methods for autonomous aerial vehicles, that involve mapping and path planning using Grids and Graphs. We are working on including Velocity Planning into our pipeline right now.

May 2020 - May 2021

Convenor

Aeromodelling Club IIT Bombay

Part of an 8-member team in charge of publicizing and conducting club events, and maintaining club inventory. I Led the Design team of the club to design event posters, club merchandise, artwork and social media posts. I also organized and mentored teams in the PlutoX Hackathon, using the Primus Flight controller to build fun projects. Even various online events and lectures as part of the club with a virtual footfall of 100+ people. On top of that, I contributed various innovative, practical and feasible event ideas, along with budget estimates and timelines
